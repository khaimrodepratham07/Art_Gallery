About the Artist

Welcome to my creative space! I'm Pratham with a deep passion for detailed mandalas and expressive portraits. My work is rooted in mindfulness, tradition, and storytelling — each piece is a reflection of inner balance and the beauty of the human form.

With every mandala, I aim to weave intricate patterns that symbolize harmony, focus, and spiritual connection. My portraits, on the other hand, capture emotion, character, and the unique essence of every subject. Whether drawn from imagination or inspired by real life, my art invites you to pause, observe, and feel.

Thank you for visiting my gallery — I hope my work brings you as much peace and inspiration as I find in creating it.
